Burial Schemes

Thinking about your own funeral is never an easy thing to do but planning ahead can bring real peace of mind for you and your loved ones. Advance funeral planning allows you to prepare for the future. We offer various Burial Schemes which cater for you and your family.

Requirements & Information

  • I.D Photocopies of Member and Spouse
  • Birth Certificates of Children
  • Waiting Period: 6 Months
  • Covers: Member, Spouse, and children below the age of 21 years
